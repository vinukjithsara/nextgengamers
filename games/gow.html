God of War (2018) is a critically acclaimed action-adventure game that reboots the iconic franchise. This time, Kratos finds himself in the world of Norse mythology, raising his son Atreus after the loss of his wife.

Departing from the Greek pantheon and hack-and-slash style, the game adopts a more mature tone, intimate storytelling, and cinematic presentation while keeping the brutal combat the series is known for.
